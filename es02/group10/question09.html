How many times are “Malaysian Chinese” mentioned in the movies through the years?

Description Protocol Data

Description

Through this picture, we mainly want to show the relevance between the type of movies and the year. The column histogram shows the trend of the movies which “Malaysian Chinese” is mentioned through the years. For example, the Malaysian movies gradually disappeared after 2004, and then it has reappeared in recent years from 2013. It is also shown that “Malaysian Chinese” is mentioned more in movies around 2015-2016.

Protocol

Based on the data of previous section, we want to highlight the actors of the type of movies, the number of keywords mentioned and year produced to see the trend. Then we made the visualization in Rawgraphs and Illustrator.
