Based on the data from Phase 2, our conclusion is: At present, the public does not pay much attention to the data center. When searching for data center energy, the top 25 webpages（baidu or google）obtained by search engines are more suitable for decision makers in professional fields, industry organizations and decision makers at relevant governments, while social media sources of information rarely appear on the top 25 websites. This shows that the energy consumption of the data center has not attracted strong public attention. Data center is still an area that stakeholders in related industries will pay attention to.
